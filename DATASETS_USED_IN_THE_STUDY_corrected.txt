DATASETS USED IN THE STUDY

Manuscript:
Daily Drought Prediction in the Huaihe River Basin Using VMD-Informer-LSTM

1. ERA5 daily meteorological variables

Dataset name:
ERA5 post-processed daily statistics on single levels from 1940 to present

Data source / provider:
Copernicus Climate Data Store (CDS), Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S) / European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF)

Official access link:
https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/datasets/derived-era5-single-levels-daily-statistics?tab=overview

DOI:
Not provided in the manuscript. Please refer to the official access link above.

Variables used in the study:
Daily actual evapotranspiration, potential evapotranspiration, surface pressure, cloud cover, maximum temperature, mean temperature, wind speed, and precipitation.

Last access:
23 June 2026


2. Land-use data

Dataset name:
Chinese multi-period land use remote sensing monitoring dataset (CNLUCC)

Data source / provider:
Resource and Environment Science and Data Center, Chinese Academy of Sciences (RESDC)

Official DOI page:
https://www.resdc.cn/doi/doi.aspx?DOIid=54

DOI:
10.12078/2018070201

Data used in the study:
Land-use data for 2005, 2010, 2015, and 2020 with a spatial resolution of 1 km.

23 June 2026
